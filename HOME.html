WHAT'S HAPNIN?

HAPNIN' is more than just an event finder - it's your local pulse on Surabaya's vibrant scene. Whether you're a music enthusiast, a knowledge seeker, or a festival-goer, HAPNIN' ensures you never miss out on the city's best experiences.

For the Music Enthusiast

From underground gigs to stadium concerts, HAPNIN' ensures you never miss a beat. Discover new bands, international acts, and everything in between. Your next favorite performance is just a click away!

For the Knowledge Seeker

Expand your horizons with our curated selection of seminars, workshops, and talks. Whether you're into tech, business, arts, or personal development, level up your skills and connect with fellow learners.

For the Festival Goer

Experience the best festivals Surabaya has to offer! From food festivals to cultural celebrations, art exhibitions to pop-up markets - get ready to immerse yourself in unforgettable experiences.
